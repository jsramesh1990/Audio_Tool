The **Toradex Verdin** and **Colibri** System-on-Modules (SoMs) based on the **NXP i.MX 8M Plus** are high-performance embedded computing platforms designed for multimedia, AI, industrial control, robotics, and audio applications. They combine Linux-class processing, real-time control, and AI acceleration on a compact module. ([Toradex Developer Center][1])

## 1. Processor Architecture

The i.MX 8M Plus is a heterogeneous multicore SoC with three major processing domains:

### Application Processor – Quad Cortex-A53

* 4 × ARM Cortex-A53 cores
* Up to **1.6–1.8 GHz**
* 64-bit Armv8 architecture
* Runs Linux, graphical UIs, networking, and application software.

This cluster is ideal for:

* Modern DAWs (Digital Audio Workstations)
* Audio synthesizers
* Human-machine interfaces (HMIs)
* Industrial dashboards
* Media playback and streaming

The A53 cluster includes:

* NEON SIMD acceleration
* 512 KB L2 cache
* Hardware virtualization support
* Linux SMP support.
  ([Toradex][2])

---

### Real-Time Processor – Cortex-M7

The chip also includes:

* 1 × Cortex-M7F MCU
* Runs at **800 MHz**
* Can execute:

  * Bare metal firmware
  * FreeRTOS
  * Real-time DSP workloads

Typical uses:

* Audio DSP
* MIDI processing
* Motor control
* Sensor fusion
* Low-latency audio pipelines
* Industrial control loops

The M7 can run independently from Linux, allowing:

* Linux UI + networking on A53
* Real-time DSP/audio on M7

This separation avoids audio glitches and ensures deterministic latency. ([Toradex][2])

---

## 2. AI Engine (NPU)

One of the biggest advantages of the i.MX 8M Plus is its integrated:

### Neural Processing Unit (NPU)

Specifications:

* Performance:
  **2.3 TOPS**
* Dedicated hardware accelerator
* Supports:

  * TensorFlow Lite
  * ONNX
  * PyTorch converted models
  * NXP eIQ AI toolkit

Applications:

* Voice recognition
* Keyword spotting
* Speaker identification
* Beamforming
* Audio event detection
* Instrument recognition
* Noise suppression
* Vision AI

The NPU offloads AI workloads from the Cortex-A53 CPUs, dramatically reducing power consumption and latency. ([Toradex][3])

## 3. Audio Features

For audio instruments and professional sound equipment, the i.MX 8M Plus is especially attractive.

### Digital Audio Interfaces

The SoM supports:

* PCM audio

This enables connection to:

* Audio codecs
* DACs
* ADCs
* Digital mixers
* External DSPs
* Class-D amplifiers

Audio applications include:

* Digital pianos
* Guitar processors
* Effects units
* Mixing consoles
* Stage instruments
* Smart speakers
* DAWs and synths

The Cortex-M7 can handle:

* Audio filtering
* FFT
* Reverb

* Low-latency DSP

while Linux handles:

* GUI
* Preset management
* MIDI
* Networking.

## 9. Verdin vs Colibri Families

| Feature            |            Verdin |          Colibri |
| ------------------ | ----------------: | ---------------: |
| Pin count          |            Higher |            Lower |
| Performance        |              High |        Mid-range |
| AI/NPU             | Yes (i.MX8M Plus) |          Limited |
| PCIe               |               Yes | Depends on model |
| Display interfaces |          Multiple |            Fewer |
| Industrial I/O     |         Extensive |         Moderate |
| Multimedia         |          Advanced |         Moderate |
| Expansion          |         Excellent |  Compact systems |

The **Verdin iMX8M Plus** is aimed at:

* AI edge computing
* Professional audio
* Multimedia systems
* Robotics
* Machine vision
* Industrial HMIs

The **Colibri** family is more suitable for:

* Compact embedded devices
* Entry-level HMIs
* Gateways
* Simpler industrial controllers.

([Toradex Developer Center][1])

* Multiple I2S/SAI audio ports
* S/PDIF support
* 3D GPU accelerated UI
* HDMI and touchscreen displays
* Industrial Linux with Docker and OTA

makes the Verdin iMX8M Plus particularly attractive for:

* Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs)
* Synthesizers
* Guitar processors
* AI-powered smart speakers
* Mixing consoles
* Digital pianos
* Voice-controlled musical instruments
* Audio analyzers
* Industrial audio processing systems

Its heterogeneous architecture allows Linux applications, hard real-time DSP, and AI inference to run simultaneously with low latency and industrial-grade reliability. ([Toradex][2])
